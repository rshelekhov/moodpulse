# Privacy Policy for MoodPulse Bot
Effective date: February 9, 2026

## 1. Who we are
The MoodPulse bot is operated by the bot owner.
Contact: me@rshelekhov.com

## 2. What this policy covers
This policy describes how we collect and use personal data when you interact with the MoodPulse Telegram bot.

## 3. Data we collect
- Telegram account data: user ID, username, name, language.
- Check-ins: mood, energy, sleep duration/quality, anxiety, irritability, medication status, notes you choose to add.
- Settings: timezone, reminder time, alert preferences.
- Usage data: commands used, timestamps, and error logs.
- Email (optional): if you request email reports, we use the email you provide only to send the report and do not store it in our database.

Some of this information may relate to health. You choose what you share.

## 4. How we use data
- Provide the bot features (check-ins, reminders, alerts, stats, charts, exports).
- Maintain, debug, and improve the service.
- Prevent abuse.
- Send requested email reports via Mailgun.

## 5. Sharing and processors
- Telegram delivers your messages and related metadata to the bot.
- Data is stored in our database; infrastructure may be provided by hosting providers.
- Mailgun processes email addresses and attachments to deliver reports.

We do not sell your personal data.

## 6. Retention
We do not have a fixed retention period. We keep data as long as needed to provide the service. You can delete your data at any time using the /delete_me command. Residual copies may remain in backups for a limited time, if applicable.

## 7. Your choices and rights
You can stop using the bot at any time.
You can delete all your data using the /delete_me command, or contact us for access, correction, and export requests.

## 8. Security
We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.

## 9. Changes
We may update this policy. We will update the effective date and, when feasible, notify users in the bot.
